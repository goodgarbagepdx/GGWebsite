DOING OUR PART TO CLEAN UP PORTLAND

Good Garbage was founded by out of work members of my gym with the same honest hard work that gets results in competition. We try to make our care for people visible in our everyday work. We our confident that you'll see what we mean.

WHAT WE DO

We can haul away almost anything! Items such as miscelanieous household objects, furniture, construction and yard debris, and much, much more! Some items may require testing before we can take them such as old wooden window glassing, vinyl, linoleum, lath and plaster, popcorn texture on drywall, and roof tar paper

ITEMS WE DO NOT ACCEPT

Batteries

AA, AAA, D, and so on (car batteries are ok!)

Paint

Primer, first coat, flat paint, eggshell, gloss, and spraypaint

Bulbs

T12, T8, T5 tube lights as well as blacklights (LEDs and other lighting systems accepted)

Household Chemicals

Hand sanatizer, soaps, bleach, bathroom cleaners and other household chemicals
